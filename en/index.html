What are the devices that support the AIS 5G Cloud Game service ?
From now to 1st November 2020, the service is compatible with Android devices only. After 1st November 2020, the service will be ready for use on PCs, Mac computers, Huawei, Android smartphones, Android IV and Linux, with more connectable accessories to be available soon. For optimum experience, the suggested internet speed is 6 Mbps minimum. The service experience may vary according the device used.
Does the service support multiplayer mode ?
The service supports multiplayer mode for many games while some games will feature co-op mode where you can challenge your friends online. The co-op mode plus an extensive array of new games that allow multi players will be available soon.
How do I save the game data ?
The file of a game will be managed by the particular game. Your game history will therefore be saved according to how the game manages players’ game data.
Do I have to download games ?
No. Streaming technology makes it possible to play games right away, just with the AIS 5G Cloud Game service on Blacknut by GAMELOFT. What’s more is that no extra storage will be required and the app uses less than 100 MB.
What kind of controllers can be used with AIS 5G Cloud Game on Blacknut by GAMELOFT ?
Xbox One or Bluetooth controllers to connect to your smartphone are recommended.

**Some games in the catalogue are not compatible with all kinds of controllers, and this may affect your gaming experience. Information about the recommended controllers for a specific game is displayed on the game information page. Despite the fact that most games support a keyboard/mouse, controllers are recommended for your best experience
What is the minimum internet speed required for AIS 5G Cloud Game ?
AIS 5G Cloud Game is a cloud gaming platform (real-time streaming) where internet network efficiency is required to ensure a stable connection, hence a maximum gaming experience. The minimum internet speed recommended is 6Mbps. Slow internet connection may result in data loss or audio/video distortion. Upon your opening of the Blacknut by GAMELOFT app, your internet connection status shall be tested and notified.
In case a game is not correctly displayed and there is a problem, how should this be solved ?
If you are using a Wi-Fi network, try to stay as close to the router as possible. Avoid using two streaming services simultaneously or downloading a program while playing the cloud game.
Is it possible to create different profiles for different players ?
Yes, it is possible to create numerous profiles on the application. Each profile contains different favorites and saved games.
Is parental control possible ?
Yes. Parental control can be managed by creating a child’s user profile for AIS 5G Cloud Game on the Blacknut by GAMELOFT app.
How can I access AIS 5G Cloud Game on the Blacknut by GAMELOFT app ?
To use AIS 5G Cloud Game, you are required to be an AIS pre-paid or post-paid customer and get connected to the internet (3G/4G/5G). You will automatically be logged in and checked. In case you get connected through Wi-Fi, follow the instructions and confirm with an OTP by entering your phone number on the app. Then, enter the OTP received via SMS on the log-in page.
How can I subscribe to the service ?
Please go to www.ais.co.th/5G/CloudGame or press *528#call to learn how to subscribe. Once you have subscribed to the service, you have direct access to the games; otherwise, you will be asked to subscribe.
How do I fix game errors ?
Please report problems through the Blacknut by GAMELOFT app. Explain the problem found by: Step-by-step instructions / Phone number / Device / Game’s name / Current connection (* Available after 1st November 2020)
How can I cancel my AIS 5G Cloud Game
subscription ?
Before 1st November 2020, you can cancel your subscription by calling AIS Contact Center at1175. After 1st November 2020, your subscription can be cancelled directly through the application. After the cancellation, your saved data will still exist for your future access
How can I subscribe to AIS 5G Cloud Game ?
After the application download, open the application and log in. You will then be asked to subscribe.
How much is the AIS 5G Cloud Game service fee ?
The monthly service fee is 299 Baht.
In case of inaccessibility to the service, what can be the cause ?
In case your payment is made prior to the expiry date and the extension cannot be verified, please check whether your SIM has a balance of over 299 Baht, and wait a day for your validity day extension. If this does not help, you are suggested to cancel your subscription and re-subscribe.
